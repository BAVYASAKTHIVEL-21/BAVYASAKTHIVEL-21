## Hey, I'm Bavya

<img align="right" width="150" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/bavyasakthivel-21/bavyasakthivel-21/main/mascot.svg?v=1" alt="ops-bot" />

I'm a Site Reliability Engineer who likes making complex systems reliable, fast, and calm to run, the kind of infrastructure that just works and stays out of the way.

Most of my time lives around **Kubernetes**, **Terraform**, and **CI/CD**: automating the boring parts, catching problems before anyone feels them, and giving developers a smooth path from commit to production. If something can be codified, monitored, or automated, that's usually where you'll find me.
